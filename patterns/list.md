### Class Design
__SOLID__ is the acronym for a set of practices that, when implemented
together, make code adaptive to change. The SOLID
practices were introduced by [Bob Martin](http://butunclebob.com/ArticleS.UncleBob.PrinciplesOfOod) almost 15 years ago.
Even so, these practices are not as widely known as they could
be—and perhaps should be.
